# Craftable Onboarding Roadmap
## From Sign-Up to Full Visibility — 4 Weeks

---

## Week 0: Before We Start — Pickup Your Info
**Your time: ~1 hour total**

### Your To-Do:
- [ ] **Your vendor list.** Grab 5-10 vendor invoice PDFs from your inbox — Friday/Food Location/whoever — upload to the secure folder. (30 min)
- [ ] **Your contacts.** Think: who runs beverages? Who runs food? Who has the login for your POS/cashier system? (15 min)
- [ ] **Your invoice list.** One recent invoice from each major vendor so we can see what you're currently paying. (15 min)

→ **Success:** Kickoff call scheduled, vendor folder shared.

---

## Week 1: Connect & See Your Data
**Your time: ~2-3 hours total**

### Your To-Do:
- [ ] **Kickoff call** (45 min) — we'll screen-share, connect your POS, and show you sales data live.
- [ ] **Accounting connection** (30 min) — send QuickBooks/Sage login (or invite to your accounting software).
- [ ] **Invoice upload** (60-90 min) — drag & drop 90 days of invoices to the secure folder (we process them, you just upload).
- [ ] **App install** (15 min) — download Craftable app, we'll walk through it together.

### What Happens This Week:
- POS connected → sales data starts flowing immediately
- Invoices processed → we learn your vendor prices & pack sizes
- Accounting sync → bills flow directly to your books

→ **Success:** Sales data matches your POS daily totals. You can see the app working.

---

## Week 2: Learn Your Invoices
**Your time: ~3 hours total**

### Your To-Do:
- [ ] **Review mapped invoices** (45 min) — we'll prep 80% of items, you confirm the matches.
- [ ] **Fix the tricky ones** (45 min) — "vendor case of 24" vs "your count unit" — we show you how.
- [ ] **Build your storage areas** (30 min) — walk-in, dry storage, bar — whatever makes sense for how you count.
- [ ] **First inventory count** (60 min) — we'll do it together on the call. Just count what you have.

→ **Success:** All invoices processed and approved. You know how to count your stock.

---

## Week 3: Order Smarter
**Your time: ~2 hours total**

### Your To-Do:
- [ ] **Set par levels** (45 min) — minimum stock you need on hand (e.g., "always have 50 lbs chicken").
- [ ] **Place first digital order** (30 min) — "order to par" button builds your cart automatically.
- [ ] **Build simple recipes** (30 min) — sauces, preps, anything you count as "one item" but make from multiple ingredients.
- [ ] **Second inventory count** (30 min) — faster this time, unlocks your Cost Summary report.

→ **Success:** First order placed through Craftable. Second count done. Cost Summary report unlocked.

---

## Week 4 & Beyond: Find the Gaps
**Your time: ~1 hour setup, then 15 min/week**

### Your To-Do:
- [ ] **Build pour recipes** (30 min) — shots, wine-by-the-glass — so sales deduct the right amount.
- [ ] **Map POS to recipes** (30 min) — "burger" button deducts bun + patty + toppings automatically.
- [ ] **Review the "Waste Report"** (15 min) — shows where actual vs. expected don't match.

### What Happens Next:
- Reports unlock weekly: [See the full list →]
- Monthly check-ins: We review your cost %, variance, and savings.
- Ongoing: Place orders, track waste, optimize pricing.

→ **Success:** Variance identified and fixed. You're saving 2-5% on COGS.
